Urh

Urh Peček is a data analyist or a data scientist. He graduated in financial mathematics, where he found himself really interested in mathematics and its real world applications. That led him to earn a master's degree in applied statistics, which remains one of his passions and professions.


The most logical thing in the world for me is mathematics, as I prefer derivations and equations from which I can fully understand and explain the final conclusion. I consider myself a »forever student«, in terms of a person who is always striving to improve and learn new things in his profession. I'm a very aesthetic type of a person as I like to have everything nice and tidy and also always include my artistic streak into data analysis.


Most of my free time I devote to trail and mountain running and road cycling. In trail running I like longer races and training runs with a lot of elevation gain, among big mountains, where I can experience great adventure and also that little bit of "suffering" that a person needs to feel fully alive. In cycling I love winding roads, that I prefer to be climbed rather than descended.


I also love animals, I consider them my best friends. All of them. I try to save every fly, spider or insect. I also like to capture moments in the mountains, on my bike, or just in nature with my photo camera, where the center of the picture is most often my girlfriend, with whom I love spending time together on adventures in nature. From time to time I also take on a different project, one of which is a T-shirt merch: #Tokyo2020(Japan).
