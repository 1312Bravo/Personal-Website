Measure of expected goals in football and its application - Master thesis

PDF, Football, Research, Modeling, Simulation

This research is my master thesis in Applied statistics. The work deals with the measure of expected goals in the football game. The concept and the calculation of expected goals is presented both theoretically and practically. The impact of the expected goals value distribution is analyzed and its use to quantify player's or team's performance is presented. Based on the expected goals, the measure of expected points is derived and practically presented. A method for predicting the results of football games based on the Poisson distribution is presented and updated taking into account the expected goals. The prediction quality of methods and their comparison is analyzed on a practical example.
